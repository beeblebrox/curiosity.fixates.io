Curiosity

A private family productivity app by fixates.io

What is Curiosity?

Curiosity is a personal productivity application designed for the app owner and their immediate family. It connects to your Google account to help you manage your calendar and send emails — all from a single, unified interface.

How Curiosity Uses Google APIs

To provide its features, Curiosity requests access to the following Google services on your behalf:

  • Google Calendar (read & write) — Curiosity reads your calendar list and events to display your schedule, and can create, update, or delete events as you direct.
  • Gmail (send only) — Curiosity can send emails on your behalf when you ask it to. It does not read, modify, or delete any existing emails in your mailbox.

Curiosity only accesses the minimum data necessary to provide these features. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions. For full details on the specific OAuth scopes requested and how your data is handled, please see our Privacy Policy.
